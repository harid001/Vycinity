Our app saves you time and money

Using various API's our app provides the user with the best nearby deals, coupons, information, and reviews.

A major aspect to our application is it's integration with smart watches. The phone recieves high accuracy deals and forwards these notifications to your smart watch almost instantaneously. This allows for the user to get all the necessary information at a glance.

In addition, our app provides the user with a great deal of customizability. The radius for your search, the types of locations, as well as the type of information to be displayed can all be customized by the user.
